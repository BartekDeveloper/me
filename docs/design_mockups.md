# Design Mockups

## Final Merged Concept
This design combines the **Purple/Magenta** background with **Neon Green** accents.
- **Background**: Very dark purple-ish (`#1a051a`) with subtle gradients.
- **Cards**: Extremely dark backgrounds (almost black) with high transparency.
- **Borders**: Outer thin **Neon Green**, Inner **highly transparent** white (double border effect).
- **Accents**: Neon Green is the primary accent. Blue/Red/Yellow are used only for tiny details.
- **Cursor**: Glass-like follower effect (implied).
